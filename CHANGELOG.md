## Release Notes

### 1.0.0 Release Notes

This is the first stable release of AI-Powered K3 Health Promotion Media.

**Key Features:**
- Complete guide series for NotebookLM, Canva AI, and Sora
- End-to-end workflow for K3 campaign creation
- Quality assurance and accessibility standards
- Real-world examples and case studies
- Time-saving: 75-80% reduction in production time

**Known Limitations:**
- Sora availability may be limited
- Language support primarily English
- Requires Google and Canva accounts
- No API integration yet

**Getting Started:**
1. Read [README.md](README.md)
2. Review [Rule.md](Rule.md) and [persona.md](persona.md)
3. Follow [Guide 4: Integration Workflow](guides/04-integration-workflow.md)
4. Explore templates and examples
