## International Standards

### ISO 7010:2019 - Graphical Safety Symbols

**Purpose:** Standardized safety signs for workplaces

**Key Categories:**

| Shape | Color | Meaning | Example |
|-------|-------|---------|---------|
| Circle with slash | Red/White | Prohibition | No smoking |
| Triangle | Yellow/Black | Warning | Electrical hazard |
| Circle | Blue/White | Mandatory | Wear eye protection |
| Square/Rectangle | Green/White | Safe condition | Emergency exit |
| Square/Rectangle | Red/White | Fire safety | Fire extinguisher |

**Use in Materials:**
```
✓ Use ISO symbols where applicable
✓ Maintain correct colors
✓ Follow shape specifications
✓ Include text support
✗ Don't modify symbol designs
✗ Don't create custom safety symbols
```

## Indonesian Regulations

### Key Regulations (Permenaker)

**Permenaker No. 4 Tahun 2020**
- **Topic:** Occupational Safety and Health Management Systems (SMK3)
- **Content:** Risk management, hazard identification, worker participation
- **Use For:** Management system posters, training materials

**Permenaker No. 5 Tahun 2018**
- **Topic:** Occupational Safety and Health in Construction
- **Content:** Specific construction hazards, PPE requirements
- **Use For:** Construction safety posters, site guides

**Permenaker No. 2 Tahun 2022**
- **Topic:** First Aid in Workplace
- **Content:** First aid requirements, kit contents, training
- **Use For:** First aid posters, emergency guides

**Permenaker No. 30 Tahun 2015**
- **Topic:** Personal Protective Equipment (APD)
- **Content:** PPE requirements, employer obligations, worker rights
- **Use For:** PPE posters, training materials

### Legal Requirements for Communication

**Mandatory Safety Information:**
```
All Workplaces Must Display:
☐ Emergency exit routes
☐ Fire safety procedures
☐ First aid locations
☐ PPE requirements
☐ Hazard warnings
☐ Emergency contacts
☐ Reporting procedures
```

**Language Requirements:**
- Primary: Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia)
- Secondary: Local language where applicable
- Bilingual: International/migrant worker areas

## Content Requirements

### Mandatory Elements

**All Safety Materials Must Include:**
```
1. Clear Identification
   ├─ What is the hazard?
   ├─ What are the risks?
   └─ Who is affected?

2. Specific Instructions
   ├─ What to do
   ├─ How to do it safely
   └─ What NOT to do

3. Visual Support
   ├- ISO symbols where applicable
   ├─ Illustrations/photos
   ├─ Color coding
   └─ Clear typography

4. Emergency Information
   ├─ Who to contact
   ├─ Where to go
   └─ What to say

5. Additional Resources
   ├─ More information sources
   ├─ Supervisor contact
   └─ Safety department
```

### Prohibited Content

**Never Include:**
```
❌ Inaccurate safety information
❌ Outdated procedures
❌ Misleading claims
❌ Minimized risks
❌ Unauthorized modifications to standards
❌ Inconsistent with regulations
❌ Copyrighted materials without permission
```

### Language & Literacy

**Best Practices:**
```
✓ Grade 6-8 reading level
✓ Simple, direct language
✓ Active voice ("Do this" not "This should be done")
✓ Bilingual where needed
✓ Visual > text ratio (70:30)
✓ Universal symbols
✓ Consistent terminology
```
